Sponsorship Opportunities

The Hellenic Data Management Symposium (HDMS) is the annual forum for the Greek database community to present and discuss recent advances for data-intensive applications. The event attracts academic and industrial researchers, startups, and students, and has a vibrant program that includes keynotes, full presentations, posters and demos. This year, we celebrate the 20th HDMS and we expect an even greater participation than usual.

Sponsoring the conference is an excellent way to attract potential recruits, get visibility for your company and products, and be a part of the Global Greek data management research community. Please contact Mara Nikolaidou (mara@hua.gr) to sponsor the symposium.

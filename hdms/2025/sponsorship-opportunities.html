Sponsorship Opportunities

The Hellenic Data Management Symposium (HDMS) is the annual forum for the Greek database community to present and discuss recent advances for data-intensive applications. The event attracts academic and industrial researchers, startups, and students, and has a vibrant program that includes keynotes, full presentations, posters and demos.

Sponsoring the conference is an excellent way to attract potential recruits, get visibility for your company and products, and be a part of the Global Greek data management research community. Please contact Manos Athanassoulis (mathan@bu.edu) or Alkis Simitsis (alkis@athenarc.gr) to sponsor the symposium.

Welcome to the Hellenic ACM SIGMOD Chapter!
The Hellenic ACM SIGMOD Chapter was established in 2024 with the aim to bring together the Greek data management research community world-wide, promoting its scientific and educational contributions and facilitating the mentoring of the young researchers in Greece, Cyprus and the rest of the world.
